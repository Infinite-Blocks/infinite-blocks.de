Minecraft version

Infinite Blocks maps are compatible with your chosen Minecraft Java version. Conversions to Minecraft Bedrock for different platforms like Mobile, Console or the Windows 10 version are also possible.

Details

Interior

Every structure gets a decorative interior with style and purpose. The detail elements listed below come in handy here.

Armor Stands

Armorstands are editable to look like figurines in your world.

Entities

Any monster, animal or mob can be safely spread around the map as cute beautifiers. Especially with tweaks to their AI and behavior.

Custom heads

A Minecraft skull by default takes the textures of mobs but can be edited to display pretty much anything on an 8×8 canvas.

Night mode

For a whole-day experience, spot-on lighting features create a worlds ambience. Shaders or even the RTX version of Minecraft bring a world to life.

Escape proof

Stay safe with escape-proof worlds. Keep from falling into the void or succumbing to monsters.

Player Experience

Jump ‘n’ Runs
From beginner jumps to triple-neos, knowing the whole difficulty spectrum within Minecraft Jump ‘n’ Runs can be challenging.Your players would be excited about this!
Mazes
In case the Jump ‘n’ Runs appear too restless, a maze is always an interesting element to keep players busy.
